About Me

I am Petteri Tamminen, I am a bachelor of Hotel Management. I have been working for over ten years in the hotel industry. I started a part-time job in Radisson Blu – hotels where I did work as a breakfast waiter and a piccolo, carrying luggage of the big tourist groups that arrive in our hotel. From there I went to work with Cumulus hotels, where I did breakfast waitering and reception, still part time. After that I found my first full time job as a night receptionist at GLO Hotel Sello, and on that path I still remain today.

Night Manager

As a night receptionist I started working alone on my shifts, gradually moving up to becoming a Nigth Manager in Hotel Haven, where I had one colleague working under me. In this profession I learned to do a variety of things from customer service, mixing drinks, reporting various statistics to different sources over the web or through email, to learning how to lead a shift. This all has thought me a lot about customer service and handling pressure situations. Currently I am working in Clarion Hotel Helsinki, one of the biggest and busiest hotels in the city.

Where to next?

I am currently studying Business IT management at Laurea University of Applied Sciences. I have 10 years of knowledge of customer service, that I hope I can put to good use in a different environment. Now I am learning the ropes of the ICT- world. Hoping to find a trainee-job at any ICT company that would have me. I do not see my self as a coder, but more in the customer service – side of any company.
